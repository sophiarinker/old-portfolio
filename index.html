My Software Journey

2017-2021

I was a university student studying for my Bachelors of Science Honors in Zoology with a minor in Neuroscience. My first exposure to the world of coding was through R language statistical analysis which I found equally as frusterating as I did fascinating.

2021

After graduation, I began to learn about computer science through the CS50 edX Harvard course I stumbled across online. I quickly determined that my interest in building functional and asethetic software led me to the frontend of web development.

2022

While trying to determine my next steps, I decided it was time to make a move on a career in tech. Through a combination of freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, UpSkill and Meta's new professional certification in front-end development, I have been learning essential job skills and building projects whenever I can. For a complete list of my courses and certifications that I have pursued, please see my LinkedIn profile linked below. More importantly, for an in-depth look at my projects, please read on!
